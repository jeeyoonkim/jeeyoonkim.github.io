DepCo enforces transparency to Vietnamese cosmetics market

DepCo is an AI based cosmetics products recommender and information aggregator in Vietnam. It tackles a transparency issue in Vietnamese cosmetics market where it lacks official reference of the cosmetic products distribution channel and unbiased information about products. Problem Space

Business team requested for a social media like cosmetic products review platform where users can freely share their experiences, as a result, forming a credible community.

Also, the business team wanted a new logo that aligns with DepCo branding. The team emphasized the transparency, interative, and modern theme in both website and logo based with pink color.

Discovery

Costumers struggle from choosing which cosmetic products to purchase because of lack of credible and personalized information.

From user research, we identified that Vietnamese costumers seek for not only credible but also personalized information that helps them to purchase cosmetic products that best fit their skin type and issues. Without knowing what kind of source to rely on, costumers lack confidence on purchasing cosmetic products on online.
