CURE App Concept

Product Thinking ‚Ä¢ Product Design

CURE consolidates space reservation systems of
Cornell University and eases the reservation process.

Cornell University's three different space reservation systems allow users to reserve various spaces and rooms in Ithaca campus. Reserving process should be concise and fast, not overwhelming. Users reserve and wait for reservation confirmation without any confidence. CURE(Cornell University Reservation Express) consolidates three existing systems and simplifies the reservation process for more delightful reservation experience.

Role: Product Manager & Product Designer

Team: 3 Product Managers

Period: Nov 2019 - Feb 2020

Result: iOS Application Prototype

PEOPLE PROBLEM

As an active member of various clubs in Cornell,
I want to reserve a space for club activities, but I cannot because current room reservation solutions are difficult to use; instructions and policies are hard to find; and confirmation for the space requests takes too long.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Cornell University students who want to reserve a room, especially for club officers.

USER RESEARCH

Scattered instructions, interface, and no guarantee for reservation confirmation make users hard to reserve.

Three existing systems, R25, Libcal, and Cornell Spaces handle a lot of reservable spaces through different methods of reservation: submiting forms, making phone calls, sending emails, and attending board committee meeting. These solutions feel overwhelming and frustrating:

"Reservation confirmation depends on how busy the staff is" & "Multiple rounds of Google search to find instructions are uncomfortable" & "I had to submit multiple forms and emails again because the space was not available for the desired time."

There is no confidence that a space is available to reserve for desired date and time, no guideline for what information to include for reservation requests, and emails are hard to track.

IDEATION

How might we make reservation experience less overwhelming?

A streamlined reservation system with communication channel will provide users more defined reservation process and understanding of status of their requests.

FACILITATE RESERVATION PROCESS

To ease the reservation process, users must be able to check space availabilities prior to the reservation request and provide all necessary information for reservation at once to organized standard system. With clear space availabilities and form asking required information in one consolidated system, reservation experience will be much more efficient.

COMMUNICATION CHANNEL

Users must be able to easily track status of reservation and have a unique space for communication with responsible staff members.

First of all, we wrote down and made an affinity diagram on what to improve from the current reservation system:

After exploring, we focused on two objectibes: facilitate reservation process, how can we unify different reservation methods? and develop communication channel, how can we reduce the number of emails to schedule a reservation and keep track the process?

Facilitate reservation process

From the user interviews, we confirmed that users preferred clearly guided forms asking for all the necessary information to emails as a means of reserving room. Our product will have a ‚Äúform‚Äù that will be delivered to the responsible staff member. By combining the contact lists into a unified form on a single website, we seek to provide users with an organized standard webpage. Inspiration is from R25 forms.

Develop Communication Channel

Provide information on status of room reservation

To prevent from overflowing email communication, our product will present all availabilities like Libcal. Also, each request will have icons indicating its status - for example, ‚Äúreceived,‚Äù ‚Äúpending,‚Äù and ‚Äúconfirmed‚Äù. Inspirations are from Cornell student center, Libcal, status bar like Dominos and Grubhub.

New communication channel between staffs and users

Instead of email, new channel like chatbot or direct message system will be incorporated within the website.

PRODUCT OVERVIEW

CURE presents space availabilities, provides reservation status, and allows direct communication with reservation staffs.

AVAILABILITY BAR

View availabilities of selected space for each half-hour. Easy to compare availabilities and reserve spaces at desired time and date.

RESERVATION STATUS BAR

Check reservation status - whether reservation is received, being reviewed, or confirmed.

DIRECT MESSAGES

Communicate with reservation staff members with built in messenger.
